About us

Colive is a technology enabled network of fully- managed rental homes. These ready-to-move in homes are conveniently located in close proximity to IT parks and business hubs.

Designer rooms with chic designs, contemporary interiors & thoughtfully designed spaces, Colive homes are equipped with modern tech enabled safety features.

All homes are fully serviced & professionally managed while offering flexible & affordable options suitable for urban living.

Home to 20,000+ single professionals & young couples Colive Community comprises of members with diverse backgrounds, experiences with mutual interests.

Vision

To establish Colive as India’s No.1 Coliving brand offering technology enabled, fully-managed rental homes to a community of like-minded millennial with premium amenities & living spaces that inspire a sense of Collaboration.

Mission

Offer Colive residents to upgrade lifestyle with chic designs and contemporary interiors, premium amenities and hassle-free living . With a customer centric approach and real estate DNA Colive aims to build automated processes and leverage technology which will help in scaling the business. Build the brand on the power of Co- Community and social togetherness which is the core principle of Colive.

The idea behind Colive is to bring an experience that is more than a home for it's 8000+ members.
